About Us

Women in IT is a sub-association under the Business and IT Society (BITSoc) at Ontario Tech University. Women in IT is open to all, with a focus empowering and connecting women currently in or entering the IT Field. WIT aims to create an inclusive space where everyone feels welcome, while ensuring that equality exists in the IT field across all genders.
